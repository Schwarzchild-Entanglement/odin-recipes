Creativity!

To achieve a feat as monumental as General Relativity, one needs creativity and originality

The following steps will lead to development of a creative and original minds one of Einstein's:

  1. Wonder about how nature works
  2. Think deeply about problems with laws of nature
  3. Be deliberate in these thinking sessions
  4. Devise some thought experiment to check the laws of nature
  5. Arrive to a radical solution from these thought experiments
  6. And last but no least, don't conform to existing ideas about nature
