Math Needed

Now that the physical principles are developed, follow the following steps to get to the math needed for relativity

  1. Apply Physical principles to Euclidean space
  2. Find out that the equations don't work in flat euclidean coordinate system so you need a coordinate system that deals with curved surfaces
  3. Also find out that you need 4 dimensions,three of space and one of time (Hail Minkowski)
  4. Differential Geometry deals with higher dimensional curved surfaces while Tensor calculus deals with higher dimensional coordinate transformation.
  5. Use use these and voila! You arrive at Einstein Field Equations that shows the evolution of Geometry of Space and Time.
